# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in PoultryGuard AI welcoming, respectful, and productive for everyone, regardless of background, identity, experience level, or location.

## Expected Behavior

- Use welcoming and inclusive language.
- Respect differing viewpoints and experiences.
- Give and receive constructive feedback with care.
- Focus on what is best for the project and its users.
- Show empathy toward farmers, researchers, maintainers, and contributors.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks
- Discriminatory language or conduct
- Public or private intimidation
- Publishing private information without consent
- Disruptive behavior that prevents healthy collaboration

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, commits, issues, or pull requests that violate this Code of Conduct. Repeated or severe violations may result in temporary or permanent exclusion from project spaces.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces, including issues, pull requests, discussions, documentation, events, and related communication channels.
