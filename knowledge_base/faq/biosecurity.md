# FAQ: Biosecurity

## Question

What are the minimum biosecurity rules every poultry farm should enforce every day?

## Answer

Control farm entry, disinfect boots and equipment, and restrict visitors to essential people only. Keep new birds in quarantine before mixing and separate clean and dirty work areas. Remove dead birds daily, maintain rodent control, and clean and disinfect feeders, drinkers, and shared tools routinely. Record visitor movement, mortality, and sanitation tasks so problems are detected early. Consistent daily biosecurity is one of the most effective ways to prevent costly outbreaks.
