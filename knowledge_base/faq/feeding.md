# FAQ: Feeding

## Question

How can I know if feeding and watering are the main cause of poor flock performance?

## Answer

Check daily feed intake, water intake, growth or egg output, and flock uniformity together. When birds eat less, drink less, grow slowly, or produce fewer eggs without clear disease signs, feeding or water management is often a major cause. Inspect feed freshness, storage conditions, and feeder access, then check water cleanliness, pressure, and availability. Sudden changes after a new feed batch or water source should be treated as high priority and corrected quickly. Keep records so you can compare trends and identify problems early.
