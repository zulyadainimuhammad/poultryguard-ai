# FAQ: Vaccination

## Question

What are the most important vaccination practices that keep poultry vaccines effective?

## Answer

Use a clear vaccine schedule by bird type and age, and do not skip booster doses. Keep vaccines in the cold chain at 2–8 °C, protect them from sunlight, and use them within recommended time after opening. Apply vaccines with the correct route and equipment, and ensure even flock coverage. Record vaccine name, batch, date, route, and house for every administration. If disease appears in a vaccinated flock, review handling, timing, and biosecurity with a veterinarian because poor storage or poor application can reduce protection.
